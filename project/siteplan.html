Mental Awareness Website Plan

Site Name

Mental Awareness

This name was chosen because it directly reflects the goal of the website - promoting awareness and understanding of mental health. It's simple, meaningful, and easy to remember.

Optional domain availability: mentalawareness.org

Site Purpose

The website will be a helpful resource for people seeking to improve or learn more about their mental health. It will offer self-care tips, ways to manage stress and anxiety, and provide connections to support networks and resources. The aim is to promote mental wellness and help users feel supported and understood.

Scenarios

  • How can I recognize the early signs of mental health challenges in myself or others?
  • Where can I find free or affordable mental health support resources near me?

Color Schema

The website will use calm and reassuring colors to create a peaceful and supportive feeling.

  • Soft Blue (#A7C7E7): Used for the background and buttons to create a calming effect.
  • Deep Purple (#6A5ACD): Used for headings and accents to emphasize importance and trust.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Georgia - for headings to give a trustworthy and professional tone.
  • Body Font: Arial - for regular text to ensure clarity and readability.
