Support Resources in South Africa

If you or someone you know is struggling, there are places you can turn to for help. Here are some trusted organizations and contact numbers you can use right now.

Emergency and Helplines

  • SADAG (South African Depression and Anxiety Group): 0800 567 567 or SMS 31393
  • Lifeline South Africa: 0861 322 322 - Free 24/7 counseling and crisis support
  • Suicide Crisis Helpline: 0800 12 13 14
  • Childline South Africa: 0800 055 555 - For children and teens in need

Free Online Support

  • SADAG Official Website - Mental health info, webinars, and help lines
  • Lifeline South Africa - Counselling services & trauma support
  • Childline SA - Support and safety for children

Find a Local Therapist

Use directories like TherapyRoute.com or contact your local clinic or GP to connect with a registered psychologist or counselor in your area.

University & School Support

Most universities in South Africa offer free mental health counseling. Ask your student services or wellness center about available support.
